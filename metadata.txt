about=Conductor is an FTTP network design and fibre-planning plugin for QGIS. It covers build-area planning, passive infrastructure (ducts, chambers, poles, PIA/aerial), fibre assignment with a cascade-aware splitter engine, optical-budget and route validation, and engineer-ready outputs (BoM, splice plan, single-line diagram) — all stored in a portable GeoPackage. Built around UK operator conventions including the Gigaloch HLD labelling specification. Survey, wayleave, build-progress and customer-connection lifecycle layers are planned future scope, not yet implemented.
